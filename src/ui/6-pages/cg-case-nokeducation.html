Natur & Kultur is a book publishing company with a long heritage of education for both school and general public. We were asked to help them design a way to publish and use their material in a digital way. The result is a digital platform where you can both read and do related assignments as a student. We also designed a way for teachers to plan their classes and also small tasks like raising your hand when you want help.
